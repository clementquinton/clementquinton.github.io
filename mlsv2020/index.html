Location and Travel Information

The seminar will take place in Villeneuve d’Ascq, close to University of Lille scientific campus. We will stay in the Inria building B, but on arrival, you will have to register at the reception desk of building A.

By car

Take the highway to Lille, then follow Villeneuve d’Ascq when indicated. Get off at the “4 Cantons” exit. Inria is at 40 Avenue de Halley, 59650 Villeneuve d’Ascq.

By train

If you are traveling by train, you will arrive either at Lille Flandres station or Lille Europe station. Located just 400 metres from one another, Lille’s two train stations are located in the city centre and are served by both the subway and the tram.

  • From Lille Europe: Take subway line 2 (the red one) in direction of St Philibert, get off at the next station which is Gare Lille Flandres.
  • From Lille Flandres: Take subway line 1 (the yellow one) to “4 Cantons Stade”.

Once at 4 Cantons, either:

  • follow this path (900m - 10 minutes walk)
  • or take bus 66 (included with your metro ticket) to “Centre Commercial (Lys-lez-Lannoy)” and get off at “Verger” station. Inria is nearby (check this view from the “Verger” bus station).

By plane

  • Shuttle services will take you to the Lille Flandres station in 20 minutes. Single ticket: 8 €, Return ticket: 10 €.
  • Catch a cab and ask for Inria Lille-Nord Europe.
